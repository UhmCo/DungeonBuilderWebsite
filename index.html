Project Dungeon Builder

Build the dungeon as you explore it

Early Development

A new kind of dungeon crawler

Place rooms like dominoes, but where you place them changes everything. A library becomes a spell-battle arena on red tiles, or a potion garden on green ones. You have 100 lives to escape before your soul joins the dungeon forever. It's Tetris meets Hades.

Dynamic Room System

Every room placement transforms the gameplay. The same room creates entirely different challenges based on where you place it. Your choices multiply possibilities and create emergent strategies.

Corruption Clock

Time is your enemy. Every minute in the dungeon makes the final boss stronger. Rush unprepared and you die. Take too long and the boss becomes unbeatable. Balance speed with preparation.

Harvesting

Defeat monsters and harvest resources to craft stronger weapons. Each enemy & room type provides unique materials that unlock different upgrade paths.
